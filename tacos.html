Q2 SEASONAL • CINCO DE MAYO

Glazed Carnitas & Pumpkin Seed Sauce

The Carnitas Build
2 lbs Pork Shoulder, Laranja & Lime Juice, Garlic, Oregano, Cumin, Cayenne, Honey, Brown Sugar.
The Green Sauce
Pumpkin Seeds, Cilantro, Jalapeño, Mexican Crema, Lime Juice.
The Deployment
01. The Braise: Cook pork in citrus and spices until tender. Finish with a honey-brown sugar glaze for that maximum reality crisp.
02. The Sauce: Blend the pumpkin seed mixture until it reaches ultra-smooth consistency.
03. Assembly: Load into golden tacos and top with the green sauce, fresh onion, and cilantro.
