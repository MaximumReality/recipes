Ingredients

  • 12 oz Sashimi-grade Tuna (Diced)
  • 1/2 cup Soy Sauce + 1/4 cup Yuzu
  • 2 tbsp Wasabi Mayo
  • 1 tsp Grated Ginger
  • Microgreens & Toasted Sesame Seeds
  • Crispy Garlic & Edible Flowers

Assembly Protocol

1 The Tuna Matrix Marinate cubes in soy, yuzu, and ginger. Place in a neat row on the left side of the plate. Alignment is key.
2 Wasabi Cloud Dollop wasabi cream next to the tuna. Think "abstract art," not "messy kitchen."
3 The Crunch Factor Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds and crispy garlic over the cream. This is where the magic happens.
4 Green Sector Toss microgreens with yuzu and place on the opposite side. Balance the plate.
