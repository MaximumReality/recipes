EXOTIC • MICHELIN STAR

Mochkil's Golden Opulence

Essential Components
Scallops, Truffle Oil, 24K Gold Dust, Foie Gras, Champagne.
The Transmission Instructions
01. The Sear: Sear 12 large scallops in truffle oil. Finish with unsalted butter until golden.
02. The Opulence: Season and sear Foie Gras slices. Dust with 24K edible gold while warm.
03. The Finish: Assemble on a bed of truffle risotto, drizzle with champagne reduction, and wrap in gold leaf.
