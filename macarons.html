Secret Macaron Tutorial

The Squad (Ingredients)
1.5 cups Almond Flour, 1.5 cups Powdered Sugar, 3 AGED Egg Whites, 0.5 cup Granulated Sugar, Pink Food Coloring.
The Pipeline
STEP 01: SIFT & PREP Sift dry data to remove lumps. Whip egg whites until frothy.
STEP 02: UNITE & COLOR Gradually inject granulated sugar until stiff peaks form. Add food coloring (Azul recommends Pink).
STEP 03: MACARON MAGIC Fold gently. Pipe onto parchment. Tap tray to release air bubbles. Rest 30 mins, bake 15-20 mins @ 300¬∞F.
